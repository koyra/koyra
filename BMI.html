BMI

BMI(body mass index) is a calculated number representing a person's level of fat or obesity level.

BMI levels are broken down by weight range and are as follows:

  • BMI < 18.5 is Underweight
  • BMI 18.5-24.9 is Normal weight
  • BMI 25-29.9 is Overweight
  • BMI 30-39.9 is Indicates obesity
  • BMI > 40 is Indicates morbid obesity

The formula to calculate BMI requires information about a person is height in meters and weight in kilograms.
